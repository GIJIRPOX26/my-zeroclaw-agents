# Real Estate Analyzer

## Problem
Most buyers do not know what they can actually afford.
They rely on rough estimates or emotion.

## Solution
A structured affordability engine that:
- calculates monthly payment
- evaluates debt-to-income ratio
- classifies risk as safe, stretched, or risky

## Output
- Monthly payment
- DTI
- Safe budget
- Verdict
- Recommendation

## Why it matters
This tool replaces guesswork with a clear decision framework.

## Stack
- Python
- Flask
- Custom affordability logic

## Status
Working prototype tested locally.
